# EP001 Practice｜Who Are You?

## 1. Shadowing 跟读

每天 5 分钟，连续 3 天：

```text
Who are you?
I am Anna.
I am Max.
You are an owl.
I love owls.
I love to walk.
```

步骤：

1. 听一句，暂停。
2. 看文本读一句。
3. 不看文本再读一句。
4. 录音，对比 VOA 原音。

## 2. Dictation 听写

听视频 00:30–01:08，写出你听到的句子：

```text
1. ______________________________
2. ______________________________
3. ______________________________
4. ______________________________
5. ______________________________
```

参考答案：

```text
Hi, welcome to my treehouse.
I am Anna.
And I am Max.
That is Max.
Max is a mailbox.
```

## 3. Sentence Substitution 替换练习

### A. I am ...

```text
I am Bob.
I am a learner.
I am a Bilibili content creator.
```

自己写 3 句：

```text
1. I am __________________.
2. I am __________________.
3. I am __________________.
```

### B. I love ...

```text
I love owls.
I love English.
I love to walk.
```

自己写 3 句：

```text
1. I love __________________.
2. I love __________________.
3. I love to ________________.
```

## 4. Speaking Task 口语输出

用 20–30 秒回答：

> Who are you?

参考结构：

```text
Hello. I am ____.
I am a ____.
I love ____.
I love to ____.
Nice to meet you.
```
