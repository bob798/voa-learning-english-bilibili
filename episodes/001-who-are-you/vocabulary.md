## 2. Sentence Patterns to Memorize

### Pattern A: Who are you?

```text
Who are you?
I am Anna.
I am Max.
```

自己替换：

```text
Who are you?
I am ______.
```

### Pattern B: You are ...

```text
You are Anna.
You are an owl.
```

注意：

- `a` 用在辅音音素前：a mailbox
- `an` 用在元音音素前：an owl

### Pattern C: I love ... / I love to ...

```text
I love owls.
I love to walk.
```

自己替换：

```text
I love ______.
I love to ______.
```

## 3. Pronunciation / Shadowing Focus

| Expression | Focus |
|---|---|
| Who are you? | 连读：Who-are-you 听起来很连贯 |
| I am Anna. | `I am` 可以缩成 `I'm`，但本集慢速使用完整形式 |
| an owl | 注意 `an` + 元音开头词 |
| I love to walk. | `love to` 中 /v/ 到 /t/ 要清楚 |
